About

Me

Hey there! I'm Gabriel Doronio Oprin

I'm a PHP junior backend developer who loves working with Laravel to build powerful APIs. Most of my experience revolves around creating the "engine" that drives web applications - the part users don't see and appreciate but definitely feel when it works smoothly.

What I Do

My main focus is developing robust APIs that connect systems and make applications work seamlessly. I've worked on projects involving RESTful APIs and have experience with single-page applications. While I primarily identify as a backend developer, I sometimes venture into fullstack territory. I'll be honest - UI design isn't my strongest suit (I usually reach for frontend libraries to help me out), but that just means I really appreciate good frontend developers!

My Approach

I believe in writing clean, maintainable code that makes life easier for everyone involved. Documentation matters to me, and I'm always looking to improve my skills and learn new technologies.

Currently, I'm expanding my knowledge in vanilla PHP, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript because I feel there's too much "magic" in the tools I'm currently using, and it feels like I've skipped over important abstractions and overall fundamentals. Additionally, I'm learning game development as a hobby so that I won't get burned out with coding, which I'm pretty excited about.
